About Me

EST. 2021 — 2026
Built From the Ground Up

Hi, I Am Taqi Raza

I never planned on becoming a barber. It all started when I tried cutting my own hair and completely messed it up. After that, I picked up the clippers again and gave my brother a haircut. Then came friends, friends of friends, and before I knew it, I was cutting hair almost every week.

As I improved, I started charging for my cuts and reinvested every dollar back into my setup. Little by little, I upgraded my tools, learned new techniques, and kept pushing myself to get better. That same mindset is something I still bring to every appointment today.

I'm currently studying Accounting and Finance at Toronto Metropolitan University, but barbering has always been more than just a side hobby for me. It's something I've built from the ground up through consistency, patience, and a genuine passion for the craft.

Whether you're coming in for a fresh fade, a lineup, a taper, or a beard trim, my goal is simple: to make sure you leave the chair looking sharp and feeling confident.
